Standard of Play (Solo)

Standards for Competitve ladder play have to be structured right,
that's why the RDL is committed to fair play, with likeable rules for all.
The RDL firmly believes that a well-structured framework is essential
to maintain a level playing field. It recognizes the importance of fair play
and endeavors to establish a set of rules that are not only comprehensive but
also amenable to all participants.
